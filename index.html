Capture the ennemy's headquarter

Where does it come from ?

Everybody has played video games during their childhood, and some are more memorable than others. For us "Advance Wars: Dual Strike" is one of those games.

"Advance Wars: Dual Strike" is a military turn-based tactics game. Using some existing mechanics from the original game, we want to propose a pleasant military tactical game, with new features to obtain a refreshing result "GlobalWarfare".

What will it be about ?

The storyline of the game

In a world in an alternative dimension, four kingdoms confront each other, fighting for power and freedom.

  • Mul, the underwater kingdom, place of ancient monsters, forgotten of all but waiting in the abyss to eat their enemies.
  • Bul, the underworld, kingdom of the dead. They want to suppress all living beings.
  • Baram, the land of angels. They were once worshiped by all, but in a godless world, what could the messenger do ?
  • Jigu, the classic living beings. They were living peacefully, taking advantage of what the earth could offer. But when attacked they can only respond and defend themselves.

These four kingdoms fought for decades, and there were many losses, too many. To ensure a life after the war, four heroes have to travel to an other dimension. There, they will fight, using the same weapons, and the winner will come back to bring peace in their world. Where does the battle of our heroes take place ? In a place looking strangely like our earth...

How will it work ?

The system of the game

Two to four players will battle each other on 5 different maps with multiple units :

  • Infantry
  • Bazooka
  • Snipers
  • Recon Jeep
  • Tank
  • Big tank
  • Interceptor plane
  • Apache Helicopter
  • Missile Battery
  • Supplier

One player can have up to 150 units at the same time on the battleground.
Furthermore players will be able to take control over cities and factories, which will increase their resources.
The game ends when there is only one player standing, if all the enemies headquarters have been captured or if everyone else forfeited.
To win a game of GlobalWarfare, you will need skill and anticipation based on the resources you have and will be likely to have in a few turns : since each controlled building enhances the amount of money earned at each turn, it is very interesting to take time to capture buildings, but it is dangerous to focus on such an objective. You will have to take the best out of your units and to know where and when to use each troop so that it is the most efficient : each troop is useful in its way.
The game will also have a map editor tool attached to it so that the players will be able to create and play on their own maps.
An idea that remains to develop, is the integration of a configuration file allowing players to use custom skins for every textures of the game. If this feature is implemented, it will be at the very end of the game development


For the moment, the game will not have an AI, due to the complexity of development for such a sophisticated AI, and will only be Player vs Player based.
The game will also have a save system so that players will be able to save their game and continue it later since a game can be very long if each players are equally skilled.
